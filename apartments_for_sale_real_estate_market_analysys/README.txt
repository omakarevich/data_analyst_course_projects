Real estate market analysis.

Determine the market value of real estate objects and typical parameters of apartments.

Research analysis and data preprocessing for a dataset with advertisements for the sale of apartments in St. Petersburg has been carried out.

The role of the area, ceilings, the number of rooms, the date of post
 in all the presented settlements and the center of St. Petersburg checked.

Market value was determined based on data from real estate site:

- real estate objects of different types, 
- typical parameters of apartments, 
- distance from the center. 

Data preprocessing has been done. Added new data.

Constructed histograms, boxplots, scatter diagrams.
