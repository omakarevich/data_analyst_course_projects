Bank Data Analysis.

Based on statistics on the solvency of clients, investigate whether the marital status and the number of the children affect the fact of the loan repayment on time.

The data was cleaned from outliers, omissions and duplicates, data types were converted and lemmas highlighted.

The share of creditworthy clients was determined.

The influence of marital status and the number of children on the fact of loan repayment on time has been analyzed.

A model of credit scoring is built - a special system that assesses the ability of a potential borrower to repay a loan to a bank.
